The martyrs

Rafah

Located in the southern Gaza Strip, Rafah has become a focal point of the humanitarian crisis. The city, which traditionally served as a vital crossing point with Egypt, now faces unprecedented challenges as thousands of displaced Palestinians seek refuge within its boundaries.

Deir al-Balah

Deir al-Balah, located in central Gaza, has become a crucial shelter for thousands of displaced families. The city's infrastructure and resources are severely strained as it attempts to accommodate the influx of people seeking safety from nearby conflict zones.

Khan Younis

Khan Younis, one of Gaza's largest cities, has experienced significant impact from the ongoing crisis. The city's healthcare facilities are overwhelmed, and basic necessities are in critically short supply as it struggles to support both its resident population and displaced persons.
